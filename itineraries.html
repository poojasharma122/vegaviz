Itineraries

Plan smarter. Skip the guesswork. See Vegas right.

These trip plans are built around how Vegas actually works. No bloated schedules. No fake "must-sees." Each itinerary is designed for a different kind of traveller — from party weekends to relaxed stays and longer escapes — with real pacing, hotel pairings, and local picks that actually help.

3-Day Party Weekend

For: Groups, birthdays, bachelor/ette trips

Focus: Nightclubs, dayclubs, rooftop bars, Strip energy

Day 1:

  • Check into The Cosmopolitan or Resorts World

  • Casual rooftop drinks at Beer Park

  • Dinner at STK or Beauty & Essex

  • Club: XS at Encore or Marquee at Cosmo

  • Late-night slice at Secret Pizza

Day 2:

  • Brunch at Catch or Bacchanal Buffet

  • Pool party: Encore Beach Club or Marquee Dayclub

  • Reset break, then dinner at Carversteak

  • Club: Omnia or Hakkasan

Day 3:

  • Smoothie + coffee

  • Uber to Downtown

  • Bar hop: Commonwealth, We All Scream

  • Dinner at Barry’s or Carson Kitchen

  • Pack up and go

3-Day Couples Getaway

For: Two-person trips, date nights, and slow mornings

Focus: Rooftop drinks, spa time, boutique hotels

Day 1:

  • Check into NoMad or The Venetian

  • Drinks at Chandelier Bar or Skyfall Lounge

  • Dinner at Lago (Bellagio Fountains view)

  • Light cocktails or lounge nigh

Day 2:

  • Morning spa session at Waldorf or A

  • Lazy pool afternoo

  • Wine tasting at Ada’s or dinner at é by José Andrés

  • Catch a Cirque show or live music

Day 3:

  • Brunch at Sadelle’s or Tableau

  • Walk Bellagio Gardens or relax by the pool

  • Smooth exit before the chaos starts again

3-Day First-Timer’s Guide

For: Vegas rookies who want the big hitters

Focus: The Strip, casinos, sightseeing, classic eats

Day 1:

  • Stay at The Venetian or Aria

  • Walk the Strip, see the Fountains, visit Caesars

  • Dinner at Hell’s Kitchen

  • Nighttime view from the STRAT tower

Day 2:

  • Buffet breakfast

  • Explore Fremont Street and Downtown

  • Ride the High Roller or STRAT rides

  • Club night: Omnia or Tao

Day 3:

  • Brunch, souvenir run, roulette goodbye round

  • Airport drop

5-Day Food & Culture Trip

For: People who want more than casinos

Focus: Local eats, art, downtown energy, live shows

Day 1:

  • Settle in at Park MGM or Virgin

  • Explore Eataly, dinner at Best Friend

Day 2:

  • Breakfast at Peppermill

  • Neon Museum and Mob Museum

  • Dinner at Esther’s Kitchen

  • Show: Absinthe or Atomic Saloon

Day 3:

  • AREA15 for immersive exhibits

  • Lunch at Tacos El Gordo

  • Art walk + cocktails at Velveteen Rabbit

Day 4:

  • Vintage shopping or brewery tour

  • Dinner at Lotus of Siam

  • Rooftop drink at Legacy Club

Day 5:

  • Brunch, pool time, casual lunch, and out

5-Day Group Trip Blueprint

For: Groups of 4+ needing space, options, and flow

Focus: Shared hotel setups, casual food, nightlife, flexible pacing

Day 1:

  • Check into Vdara, The Cosmopolitan, or Signature at MGM

  • Dinner at Momofuku

  • Bar crawl or light club night

Day 2:

  • Pool party at Encore or Wet Republic

  • Dinner at Carverste

  • Club: XS or Zouk

Day 3:

  • Golf at Bali Hai or Red Rock Canyon es

  • Chill dinner at Ellis Island

  • Fremont bar crawl

Day 4:

  • Brunch at The Henry

  • Free afternoon

  • Club: Hakkasan or Jewel

Day 5:

  • Group brunch, drop bags, head home

5-Day Relaxed Vegas

For: Visitors who want the Vegas experience — minus the chao

Focus: Pools, spa, quiet restaurants, walkable days

Day 1:

  • Check into NoMad, Vdara, or Virgin

  • Dinner at Catch, early rooftop cocktai

Day 2:

  • Spa day at Sahra or Waldorf

  • Poolside lunch, soft dinner at Lago

  • Light nightcap or skyline view

Day 3:

  • Visit Bellagio Gardens or Seven Magic Mountains

  • Lunch at Giada or Lotus of Siam

  • Art walk or lounge evening

Day 4:

  • Light breakfast, shop, nap

  • Sunset drinks, dinner at Sparrow + Wolf

Day 5:

  • Coffee run, walk, easy check out

7-Day Do-It-All Week

For: The traveller who wants it all — Strip, Downtown, clubs, pool, shows

Focus: Balanced pacing, sightseeing, nightlife, variety

Day 1:

  • Check in, get settled, casual Strip walk + dinner

Day 2:

  • Pool morning, casino time, Bellagio show

Day 3:

  • Club night: Omnia or XS

  • Late start next day

Day 4:

  • Downtown day: Mob Museum, food hall, murals

Day 5:

  • Spa day or Red Rock hike

  • Rooftop dinner

Day 6:

  • Shopping, final club night

Day 7:

  • Brunch and bounce

7-Day Work & Play

For: Remote workers extending a trip or working from Vegas

Focus: Quiet hotels, cafés, evening activities

Day 1:

  • Check into Vdara, Virgin, or Conrad

  • Setup and casual dinner

Day 2:

  • Work from café or lounge

  • Pool in the afternoon

  • Evening drink and light show

Day 3:

  • Morning meetings

  • Explore Downtown or AREA15

  • Dinner at Esther’s or Barry’s

Day 4:

  • Focused work block

  • Wellness evening: spa or yoga

Day 5:

  • Day trip: Valley of Fire or Boulder City

Day 6:

  • Open day

  • Club or Cirque show at night

Day 7:

  • Brunch, check out, reset
