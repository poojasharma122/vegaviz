About

Built for travellers who don’t want to waste time in Las Vegas

Vega Viz was created to give real travellers the tools, insights, and planning support they actually need when visiting Las Vegas. No clickbait lists. No recycled reviews. No sales pitch behind every “tip.”

We’ve spent enough time in Vegas to know what works — and what really doesn’t. From overpriced buffets to overhyped hotel rooms, confusing Strip layouts to club nights that don’t go as planned, we’ve made the mistakes so you don’t have to.

This site is about clarity, value, and smart planning. Whether you're here for a weekend blowout, a poker run, a solo recharge, or a family trip that actually works — we build guides, hotel breakdowns, and itineraries designed to save you time, money, and frustration.

What You’ll Find Here

– In-depth hotel reviews by trip type (not just star ratings)

– Real-world itineraries based on pace, interests, and budget

– Travel tips from people who’ve actually stayed, played, and walked the Strip

– Journal posts that focus on timing, costs, and what not to do

– Smart breakdowns of casino hotels, family hotels, adults-only spots, and more

No recycled content. No SEO-stuffing. No 20-page blog posts that say nothing.

Why We Built Vega Viz

We got tired of websites that felt like travel brochures — full of generic praise and no actual help. Vegas is a fast-moving city. What worked last year might not work next month. And if you land unprepared, you’ll pay for it — in time, in money, or in a hangover that hits way harder than it should.

This site exists to fix that. To show you how to avoid the traps, find the fun, and build a trip that fits your style — not someone else’s sponsored itinerary.

Who It's For

– First-timers who want to hit the ground running

– Return visitors ready to explore beyond the basics

– Solo travellers, couples, party crews, and familie

– Anyone who wants their Las Vegas trip to be sharp, smooth, and memorable for the right reasons
