Best Hotels in Las Vegas for Business Travel

Reliable Wi-Fi, calm energy, and easy access to the Convention Center.

Las Vegas is one of the top business and event destinations in North America. Whether you're attending a convention, hosting meetings, or extending a work trip into the weekend, these hotels deliver solid infrastructure, smooth logistics, and enough calm to stay productive.

Vdara Hotel & Spa

Vdara is non-gaming, non-smoking, and extremely well positioned just behind the Strip. Rooms are modern suites with kitchenettes, making it a great pick for longer stays. There's no casino noise, the lobby is quiet, and the location gives you fast access to Aria, Bellagio, and Park MGM.

Conrad Las Vegas at Resorts World

Part of the multi-brand Resorts World complex, Conrad is the most business-friendly of the three. Rooms are crisp and clean, with comfortable desks and smart features. The lobby is calm, check-in is quick, and you’re within walking distance of the Convention Center. Plus, you have access to all the dining in the complex.

Virgin Hotels Las Vegas

Off-Strip, modern, and refreshingly laid-back. Rooms come with strong Wi-Fi, plenty of workspace, and the property has multiple quiet lounges and casual meeting spots. Free parking, no resort fees, and reliable service make it one of the best options for remote work or low-key business trips.

Four Seasons Hotel Las Vegas

For high-end business stays, Four Seasons sits on the top floors of Mandalay Bay, with its own entrance and private atmosphere. It’s removed from the casino floor, has direct access to the convention space, and offers true five-star service. Ideal for executives or business travellers who want the Strip without the chaos.

Things that matter when you're working in Vegas:

– Fast elevators and quiet floors

– Strong Wi-Fi and good in-room desks

– Non-casino lobbies for easy meetups

– Quick access to Uber pickup zones

– Business centres or lounge space to recharge

Whether you're in town for CES, NAB, or a quarterly meetup, these hotels help you stay focused — and still enjoy a solid dinner when you're off the clock.
