Personal Data Policy

At Vega Viz, we are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data with transparency and care. This Personal Data Policy outlines how we collect, store, use, and protect any personal data you provide while using vegaviz.com (the “Site”).

1. What Personal Data We Collect

We only collect personal data when voluntarily provided by users, such as:

  • Email address (e.g., when signing up for our newsletter or contacting us)
  • Name (optional, when submitted via contact form)
  • Message content (only when you choose to contact us directly)

We do not collect sensitive personal information (e.g., ID numbers, payment data, addresses).

2. How We Use Your Personal Data

We use your personal data solely for the purposes it was submitted, including:

  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Sending you requested communications (e.g., newsletter updates)
  • Improving our website content and performance
  • Ensuring site integrity and security

We do not use your personal data for profiling, targeting, or behavioural tracking.
We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third parties.

3. Data Retention

We only retain your personal data for as long as is necessary:

  • Email addresses for our newsletter are retained until you unsubscribe
  • Contact form submissions are stored securely and reviewed within 60 days, then deleted if no longer relevant

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 5 below).

4. Data Protection & Security

All personal data is stored securely using encrypted systems and access controls. Only authorised team members have access to submitted data. We take technical and procedural steps to protect data from loss, theft, unauthorised access, or misuse.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have full control over your personal data. You may at any time:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for communications
  • Request that we do not process your data in certain ways

To exercise any of these rights, please email: info@vegaviz.com.
We will respond to all requests within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Our Data Principles

  • Minimalism: We only collect data we truly need
  • Consent: We only store or use personal data you choose to share
  • Transparency: You’ll always know how and why your data is used
  • Security: We protect what you give us with appropriate safeguards
  • No selling: Your data is never sold, shared, or monetised

7. Questions or Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle your personal data, we want to hear from you.
Contact us at: info@vegaviz.com
