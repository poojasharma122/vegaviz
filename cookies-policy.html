Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how Vega Viz (“we”, “our”, or “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies on vegaviz.com (the “Site”).

By continuing to use the Site, you consent to the use of cookies as described below. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, phone) when you visit a website. They help websites function properly and provide information on how users interact with them.

Cookies can be:

  • Session cookies – deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies – remain on your device until they expire or are manually deleted

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the site works smoothly and loads correctly
  • Analyse traffic and visitor behaviour using tools like Google Analytics
  • Monitor performance and identify issues (e.g., broken links, slow pages)
  • Store preferences (e.g., dismissing pop-ups or cookie notices)

We do not use cookies to track personal identities, store sensitive data, or serve targeted advertising.

3. Types of Cookies Used on This Site

Essential Cookies:
Required for basic functionality such as page navigation and access to secure areas. The site cannot function properly without these.

Performance & Analytics Cookies:
Used to collect information about how visitors use the site, which pages are most visited, and how users move through the site. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

Functional Cookies:
Used to remember user preferences (like dismissing alerts or forms) to improve user experience.

We do not use advertising or third-party marketing cookies.

4. Third-Party Tools

We may use trusted third-party services (e.g., Google Analytics) that place cookies on your device. These tools help us understand user behaviour and improve the website, but they do not collect personal data through cookies.

https://policies.google.com/technologies/cookies

5. Managing Cookies

You can choose to accept or reject cookies by adjusting your browser settings. You can also clear cookies already stored on your device.
